This is Ahmed ElDeghedy's personal growth portfolio.

He is not positioned as a generic media buyer or freelancer.

## Main goal
The website should convert founders, business owners, and marketing leads into discovery calls.

## Voice
Write as Ahmed speaking personally.
Tone: confident, strategic, human, direct.
Avoid AI-sounding language.

## Avoid visible phrases
Do not use:
- Watermarked proof asset
- Confidential Proof
- Proof Library
- Data anonymized
- AI generated
- Client identity hidden as a repeated marketing phrase

Client names must remain hidden.

Ahmed ElDeghedy | Growth Systems

## Design

Agency-level, senior growth operator, not SaaS template.
Must be responsive on mobile.

No frameworks unless explicitly requested.
